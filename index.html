哈哈智能科技發展學會

本學會成立的主要目的是以「促進產學研合作、推動科技教育創新發展」為核心宗旨，積極整合來自各界的前沿科技與教育資源，匯聚學術界、產業界與實務從業者的力量，推動智能科技應用與教育深化發展。
The primary purpose of the association is centered on "promoting collaboration among academia, industry, and research, and advancing innovation in science and technology education." It is committed to actively integrating cutting-edge technologies and educational resources from various sectors, bringing together the strengths of academia, industry professionals, and practitioners to drive the application of intelligent technologies and deepen the development of education.
